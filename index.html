RTO, Transport Authority, North Zone

The Regional Transport Office (RTO) of Delhi was established under the Department of Transport, Delhi, under section 213 of the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988. Being the capital city of India, Delhi has numerous vehicles plying on its roads every day and many more being registered every year. The RTO office of Delhi is headed by the Transport Commissioner.

F.A.Q

  • What is RFID?

    Radio frequency identification, or RFID, is a generic term for technologies that use radio waves to automatically identify people or objects. There are several methods of identification, but the most common is to store a serial number that identifies a person or object, and perhaps other information, on a microchip that is attached to an antenna (the chip and the antenna together are called an RFID transponder or an RFID.

  • How does a RFID reader work?

    A RFID system basically uses 3 components: the tag, the antenna and the reader. The tag is normally attached with an object. It contains a unique identification. The antenna detects tag, via the magnetic fields it creates. The reader receives tag information and manipulates the information. The tag information could for example be an identification number. This article focuses on the RFID reader.

  • How can I find my RFID details as I lost my phone, my customer ID and mobile no?

    You don't have to worry as the RFID is linked with the Registration Number of the Vehicle. You can easily recharge your RFID and find other details by typing your registration number in any RFID recharge supportive app.

  • How to recharge RFID tag?

    You can recharge your RFID wallet by the same methods you recharged your FASTag. Services like UPI (Paytm, Bhim, PhonePe, GooglePay), Debit or Credit cards, Net Banking, etc. can be used. Minor charges might be added as per the service you use.

  • Why is RFID system better than FASTag?

    In the FASTag system, you had to stop your vehicle and wait for the barcode to be scanned by the camera. Here in RFID system, you can just drive under the speed limit over the sensor and your RFID will be read and transaction will be done automatically.
